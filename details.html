Technology-Driven Solution for Minimizing Food Wastage

UmagineDX Hackathon Project

Problem Statement

Large amounts of edible food are wasted daily from households, hotels, restaurants, and events, while many people still suffer from hunger. The lack of a direct and real-time connection between food donors and receivers increases food wastage.

Proposed Solution

We propose a technology-driven platform that enables real-time communication between food donors and receivers. The platform ensures quick redistribution of surplus food, reducing wastage and helping needy communities efficiently.

Technologies Used

  • HTML & CSS – Frontend design
  • JavaScript – Interactivity (future enhancement)
  • GitHub – Version control & hosting
  • Cloud Platform – Deployment & scalability

Impact

  • Reduces food wastage significantly
  • Supports hunger eradication
  • Encourages community participation
  • Promotes sustainable living
